Background

I started in Electronics & Telecommunication engineering, which is where I first got comfortable tracing a signal until I understood exactly why it behaved the way it did. That habit carried straight over into backend development — these days it's SAP ABAP, chasing a bug through debugger breakpoints instead of an oscilloscope.

I work across SD, MM, FI/CO and PP/QM: custom reports, CDS views, BAdI implementations, background jobs, and the occasional archiving or compliance build. I like problems with a definite root cause, and I'd rather step through the logic myself than guess.
